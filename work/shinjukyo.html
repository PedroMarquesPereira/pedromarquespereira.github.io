Shinjūkyō2023

multi-channel video

variable duration, 2880  ×  2160 px

A collection of moving images that focus on the intrinsic qualities of light and water. Like the Shinjūkyō, the ancient bronze mirrors, these images serve a spiritual practice of exploring the potential of light as an ritualistic element. Together they form a new heterogeneous landscape, modulated by its sensorial qualities.
