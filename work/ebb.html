Ebb

2025
click image for more

three-channel video installation

11 min (looped), 2880  ×  2160 px

Bodies intertwine in a fragmented landscape, where the image does not document, but transforms. The installation proposes a sensitive study on the idea of body-landscape. A fictional place constructed from scattered fragments, captured and recomposed in layers and currents. Each body, traversed by movements or saturations, loses its original identity to give way to a new image: an affective, imagined geography, where form is shaped by its own dissolution.
