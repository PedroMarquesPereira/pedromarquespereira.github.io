Transparent figures gather and dissolve at the foot of a waterfall, their heads turned toward the rocks. Water moves endlessly, its constant rush carrying echoes of those who come and go. These ghosts walk in a space between memory and the present, their bodies fading like mist. Here, presence and absence intertwine, and seeing becomes an inherent movement of the landscape.
