No Jardim

2020
click image to watch

short film, 12 min 30 sec

HD (4:3 aspect ratio)

There is a deep affection for this place, its stillness, its openness, and its crushing emptiness. The trees, silent witnesses, hold us differently as the path beneath softens and a ritual unfolds. Through gathering and listening, we share ways of being together and alone. A feeling unlocks, not from what is shared but from this place itself.
