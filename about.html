Pedro Marques Pereira
Margarida  2024

Pedro Marques Pereira (b. 1996, Portugal) is a lens-based artist exploring the mediums of photography and video. His practice shifts between the still and the moving image, questioning their relationship while focusing on their potentiality, especially in relation to place and perception.

He is currently based in Lisbon, where he studied and primarily worked in film productions as an AC and gaffer. His work has been featured in festivals, exhibitions, and private collections. In 2022, he completed a two-month residency at Studio Kura in Japan. Presently, he focuses on developing his own projects and collaborating on cultural events.
